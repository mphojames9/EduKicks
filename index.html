About us..

EduKicks is an NPO

Formally registered in 2020 however established in 2018 owned 100% by an historically disadvantaged South African (HDSA). Darryl James who is chairing the operation the assistance of 02 sub coaches whom is in coaching, business and community projects with the requisite skills , knowledge and expertise and formally registered as an NPO.

Experience

Through its involvement in the schools and ECD community partners, it has gained unparalleled experience In delivering on educational enhancements projects, thus affording it a unique and valuable combination of experience from a local perspective.

Goals and objectives

As part of its long term strategic goals and focus, the Edukicks wants to reignite soccer development provincially and nationally with the relevant stakeholders in the education platform. Its extensive experience and commitment to operational excellence means its partners can be confident that EDUKICKS will manage their requirements safely and deliver their projects/training on time. The team consists of 02 sub coaches ( Chevlyn James and Keagan Van De Ross) and 01 head coach ( Darryl James).

Our Mission

We value encouraging and positive energy displayed by the team and are committed to the development of our kids. We believe in integrity and always ensure that we act openly and honestly in all we do. We believe in stewardship and therefore always strive to leave things better than the way we found it.
